## Basics

  * ### What is Emirates Skywards?

__

Emirates Skywards is the award-winning loyalty programme of Emirates airline and flydubai, launched in May 2000.

It offers members a range of benefits and experiences designed to complement their lifestyle and make every trip even more rewarding. As a member, you can earn and spend Miles on flights with Emirates, flydubai, and our airline partners, enjoy luxury hotel stays, plan memorable family outings, access tickets to global sporting and cultural events, and more.

Visit this [page](/english/skywards/) to know more about the programme and its exciting benefits.

  * ### Once I’ve joined Emirates Skywards, will I receive a membership card?

__

As an Emirates Skywards member you do not need to have a physical card to enjoy all the benefits of membership. Simply quote your membership number every time you transact with Emirates, flydubai or one of the Emirates Skywards partners to continue to earn and redeem Miles. You can add your digital card to your Apple Wallet, print yourself a physical copy, or save it to your device’s photo or image library for quick access to your membership details.

[Print or save your digital card](/english/skywards/account/my-profile/membership-card/) now or go to ‘My Overview’, scroll down to Quick Links, and click on Membership Card.

  * ### How can I change personal details on my Emirates Skywards account?

__

You can update your information at any time:

Through the Emirates [website](https://www.emirates.com):

    1. Log into your Emirates Skywards account
    2. Click on your name on the upper right corner go to ‘[My Overview](/english/skywards/account/my-overview/)’
    3. On the right side of the screen, you will find a section with an overview of your membership. At the bottom, click on ‘[Manage my Profile](/transfer.aspx?pageurl=/account/english/manage-account/my-profile/§ion=MYA&th=7661e082197180ace014530ebaa8446b3787f9f8)’ - update your information, including your nationality, passport number or country of issue.

Through the Emirates app:

    1. Download the app and log into your Emirates Skywards account.
    2. Go to the Skywards page and click on the 3 dots found on the upper right corner of the screen.
    3. Click on ‘Edit Profile’ and update or edit your personal details.

  * ### Why do I need to verify my email?

__

Verifying your email helps ensure that the email address you provided is valid and unique, not shared with other individual membership accounts. It also helps reduce the chances of spam and improves the security of your Emirates Skywards account. If left unverified, your account may be deactivated, or certain features may be restricted until verification is completed.

  * ### How do I verify my email address?

__

When logged in to your Emirates Skywards profile, click on the ‘Verify’ option next to your registered email address. This triggers an email via the domain emirates.email, asking you to ‘Confirm Your Email Address’. On clicking this link, you will find a ‘Verified’ flag next to the registered email under My Overview > Manage my profile > Personal details section. Note that the verification link sent via email will expire after 48 hours.

  * ### I didn’t receive a verification email. What should I do?

__

Check your spam or junk folder, as sometimes emails get filtered incorrectly. If you still can't find it, try resending the verification email by logging in to your Emirates Skywards account on www.emirates.com or the Emirates App. You will find the option to ‘Verify’ under My Overview > Manage my profile > Personal details, or you can [contact us](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/) for further assistance.

  * ### Can I change my email address after I have completed the verification process?

__

Yes, you can change your email address to a new and unique one even after verifying your current email address. You will be required to verify the new email address once you make this change.

  * ### Can I use an email address that is already registered to an existing Emirates Skywards account?

__

No, Emirates Skywards membership accounts must have a unique email address. If your email address is shared with other Emirates Skywards members, you must first update your email to a unique address and then proceed to verify. Please[contact us](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/) for further assistance.

  * ### Do I need to verify the email address for a Skysurfers account linked to my Emirates Skywards account?

__

No, as Skysurfers are linked to your Emirates Skywards account, no separate email verification is required at this stage. However, please ensure that the primary email address registered to your Emirates Skywards account is verified.

  * ### Can I merge two or more Emirates Skywards accounts?

__

Unfortunately, multiple Emirates Skywards accounts cannot be merged. Each member is allowed only one active account. If you happen to have more than one, the primary account will be retained, and the others will be closed.

If you need help identifying which account to keep, feel free to [contact us](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/) and we will be happy to assist you.

  * ### What are Skywards Miles?

__

Skywards Miles are the reward currency you earn as an Emirates Skywards member. You can earn Skywards Miles when you fly with Emirates and flydubai, as well as through our global partner network, including airlines, banks, car providers, hotels, and a range of lifestyle brands.

  * ### How long are Skywards Miles valid for?

__

Your Skywards Miles are valid for three years from the date of earning. Within the calendar year that Skywards Miles are due to expire, they will be removed from your account at the end of the month in which you were born.

For example, if you earned Skywards Miles in June 2019 and your birthday is in August, these Skywards Miles will expire on 31st August 2022.

If you have any Skywards Miles in your account that are due to expire in the next 12 months, you can set automated messages from your My Account page to remind you when Skywards Miles are due to expire.

If you have any Skywards Miles in your account that are due to expire in the next 3 months, you can pay to extend their validity for another 12 months beyond the date of the original expiry. Or if you have Skywards Miles that have expired within the last 6 months, you can also pay to reinstate their validity. Please visit this [page](/english/skywards/do-more-with-your-miles/) for complete details.

  * ### What can I do if my Skywards Miles are about to expire while I can’t travel?

__

If you’re not travelling any time soon, you can spend your Skywards Miles on rewards with our hotel, retail and lifestyle partners. Visit this[page](/english/skywards/partners/ "Our Partners") to see our full list of partners where you can make the most of your Skywards Miles.

If you are planning to travel in the future, you can also book your flights with Emirates, flydubai and our partner airlines up to 11 months ahead.

You also have the option to extend the validity of your Skywards Miles that are about to expire in the next 3 months or reinstate Skywards Miles that have expired in the last 6 months. Click [here](/english/skywards/do-more-with-your-miles/) for more information.

  * ### Where can I find out how to spend my Skywards Miles?

__

There are plenty of ways to spend your Skywards Miles. You can spend Skywards Miles on flights with Emirates, flydubai, and our airline partners. You can also spend Skywards Miles on our hotel, and retail and lifestyle partners. For more information, visit our[Spend Miles](/english/skywards/spend-miles/) page.

Use our [Miles Calculator](/english/skywards/miles-calculator/) to quickly check if you have enough Skywards Miles to redeem a flight reward with Emirates – just enter your chosen route to see the number of Miles required.

  * ### What are Tier Miles?

__

While[Skywards Miles](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/what-are-skywards-miles/) can be used to buy rewards, Tier Miles are collected to help you move up membership tiers and are earned mainly when you fly with Emirates and flydubai or on a codeshare flight that carries an Emirates flight code (EK).

The number of Tier Miles that you earn during a qualification period determines the membership tier you belong to: Blue, Silver, Gold or Platinum. 

Learn more about the advantages of each [Emirates Skywards membership tier](/english/skywards/membership-benefits/).

Your tier is updated automatically when you collect enough Tier Miles. You can view your tier status and check how many Tier Miles are required to move up a tier on the Skywards page of the app and the ‘My Overview’ page on the website, as long as you are logged in.

Learn more about [moving up to a higher tier](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/how-do-i-move-up-to-a-higher-tier/).

Learn more about [retaining your tier status](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/how-can-i-ensure-that-i-keep-my-current-tier/).

  * ### How are my Tier Miles calculated?

__

Tier Miles are calculated at the same rate as Skywards Miles; taking into account the fare you have paid, the route, and the class of travel. Please note that you can’t earn Tier Miles through our partners. Tier Miles can be earned only on Emirates flights, flydubai flights and codeshare flights marketed by Emirates but operated by another airline.

Use our [Miles Calculator](/english/skywards/miles-calculator/) to see how much you will earn on your next flight.

Learn more about [Emirates Skywards membership tier](/english/skywards/membership-benefits/).

  * ### How long are my Tier Miles valid for?

__

Tier Miles are valid for up to 13 months from the date you start earning, which is usually your first flight as an Emirates Skywards member either on Emirates, flydubai or on a codeshare flight marketed by Emirates but operated by another airline. If you receive Tier Miles from a backdated claim, they will be valid from the date of the flight.

Learn about [how to keep your tier status](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/how-can-i-ensure-that-i-keep-my-current-tier/).

  * ### Can I transfer or buy Tier Miles?

__

No, Tier Miles cannot be transferred or bought. They are only earned when you fly with Emirates, flydubai, or on codeshare flights marketed by Emirates but operated by another airline.

If you want to retain your tier status or move up a tier, consider upselling your fare brand or upgrading your cabin class on your next flight to earn more Tier Miles. You may also want to subscribe to the [Skywards+](/english/skywards/skywards-plus/subscription/ "Learn more") Premium package, which gives you 20% more Tier Miles during your subscription period.

  * ### Why is my flight not showing in My Trips?

__

Our ‘My Trips’ tool displays only your upcoming trips with Emirates. If you have a flydubai booking, you’ll need to log in at flydubai.com to view it.

Reward bookings on Emirates (flights purchased using Skywards Miles) will also appear in My Trips, and you can view them by going to the ‘[Manage your booking](/english/manage-booking/)’ page and logging in with your last name and booking reference.

Emirates flights may not show up in My Trips if:

    * The first name or last name entered at the time of the booking does not match the name in your Emirates Skywards account; for example, ‘Will’ instead of ‘William’.
    * Your Emirates Skywards membership number is not associated with the booking. To update this, please add your Emirates Skywards membership number in Manage your booking.

If you feel that none of the above applies to your future bookings, please call [Emirates Contact Centre](/english/help/ "Help and support") for assistance.

  * ### Emirates Skywards sometimes refers to origin and destination. What does this mean?

__

Your origin is the airport where you start each leg of your journey, and your destination is the airport where you end each leg of your journey. So, if you’re flying a return trip from London to Auckland, your outbound flight has an origin of London and a destination of Auckland; on your return flight, the origin is Auckland and the destination is London. Stopovers are not counted as a destination.

  * ### What is a travel coordinator?

__

A travel coordinator is someone aged 18 or older who an Emirates Skywards member can nominate to manage aspects of their account on their behalf. A nominated travel coordinator can:

    * access and obtain information from the member’s account
    * claim rewards for the member
    * amend any account information related to the member’s Emirates Skywards membership

You can nominate a travel coordinator by contacting the [Emirates Contact Centre](/english/help/ "Help and support"), or by logging in to emirates.com and submitting the form on this [page](/english/skywards/account/my-profile/travel-coordinator/ "Travel Coordinator").

For more information on the terms and conditions for nominating a travel coordinator, visit our [Programme Rules](/english/skywards/emirates-skywards-programme-rules/) and refer to Section 4: Account Management.

  * ### How does my travel coordinator access my account online?

__

Your travel coordinator will not have access to your online account unless you share your account credentials with them.

  * ### Can a travel coordinator benefit from my Emirates Skywards membership?

__

Travel coordinators are not entitled to any membership privileges from your account. However, they can always join the Emirates Skywards programme themselves to start enjoying the benefits.

  * ### What are the types of communication I can subscribe to?

__

You can subscribe to:

    1. Emirates airline news and offers
    2. Emirates Skywards news and offer
    3. flydubai news and offers

  * ### How can I subscribe to receive news, offers and fare specials?

__

You can subscribe to receive Emirates, Skywards and/or flydubai news and offers when you enrol in Emirates Skywards, or anytime later by logging in with your Skywards account and going to ‘[Manage Email Subscriptions](/english/skywards/account/my-profile/notification-settings/)’. You can also update your flydubai communications subscriptions on the flydubai website.

  * ### How can I unsubscribe from receiving any communications?

__

You can unsubscribe at any time via the Unsubscribe link found at the bottom of your flydubai and/or Emirates emails, by updating your Emirates Skywards account preferences, or by contacting Emirates or flydubai through their Live Chat or Contact Centre.

  * ### Why am I being offered to subscribe to flydubai airline news and offers in my Emirates Skywards account?

__

Emirates Skywards is the loyalty programme for both Emirates and flydubai; therefore, you have the option to choose to receive airline news and offers from both Emirates and flydubai.

  * ### I enrolled into Emirates Skywards, why am I now receiving flydubai news and offers?

__

At the time of enrolment into Emirates Skywards, you were given the option to subscribe to Emirates, Emirates Skywards and/or flydubai news and offers. Your communication preferences have been updated accordingly.

  * ### I have opted in to receive flydubai news and offers, but why am I unable to see this as a preference under my Emirates Skywards account?

__

This means that the email address you have used is associated with several Emirates Skywards membership numbers or the name you have provided does not match the name on your Emirates Skywards account. Please log in to your Emirates Skywards account and update your email subscriptions under[Personal Preferences](/english/skywards/account/my-profile/personal-preferences/).

  * ### When I subscribe to flydubai news and offers, will I receive only Emirates Skywards related information?

__

You will also receive all flydubai news and offers, including promotions from flydubai and flydubai Holidays.

  * ### What personal information is shared with flydubai when I opt in to receiving flydubai news and offers?

__

Your name and email address will be shared with flydubai in order for you to receive such newsletters. flydubai is responsible for the processing of your personal information as per[ flydubai’s privacy policy﻿ __](https://www.flydubai.com/en/information/policies/privacy-policy).

  * ### How can I delete my Emirates Skywards account or terminate my membership?

__

You can delete your Emirates Skywards account or terminate your membership at any time through:

    1. Emirates website: Log in, go to your profile, select ‘[Manage my Account](/transfer.aspx?pageurl=/account/english/manage-account/my-profile/§ion=MYA&th=7661e082197180ace014530ebaa8446b3787f9f8)’, and you will find the option to delete your account.
    2. The Emirates App: Go to the Skywards page, tap the three dots in the upper right corner, select ‘Edit profile’, and you will see the option to delete your account.
    3. [Live Chat](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/): Speak with our team and they will be happy to assist you.

  * ### What happens if I delete my Emirates Skywards account or terminate my membership?

__

If you choose to delete your Emirates Skywards account or terminate your membership, please note the following:

    * Unused Skywards Miles and rewards: All of your unused Miles and rewards, along with any benefits or privileges associated with your membership, will be immediately forfeited and rendered null and void. These forfeited Miles and rewards have no cash value and cannot be redeemed or refunded.
    * Skywards+ subscription: If you have an active Skywards+ subscription, it will be terminated without refund.
    * Linked accounts: Any linked accounts, such as Skysurfers or My Family accounts (if you are the Family Head), will automatically be terminated or de linked upon deletion of your Emirates Skywards account.
    * Business Rewards accounts: Any Business Rewards account registered using your Emirates Skywards Account credentials will no longer be accessible with those credentials. For more details, please refer to the Business Rewards terms and conditions.

  * ### Can I reverse the deletion of my Emirates Skywards account?

__

No, the deletion of your Emirates Skywards account is permanent and cannot be reversed. Once your Emirates Skywards account is deleted, all associated data, benefits, and privileges will be irreversibly removed.

## Buy, Gift, Transfer, Reinstate, Extend, Multiply Miles

  * ### Where can I buy, gift, transfer, extend or reinstate Skywards Miles?

__

For buying, gifting, and transferring Skywards Miles, you can do it through:

    * Logging in on emirates.com; or
    * Contacting the [Emirates Contact Centre](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/); or
    * Visiting the Emirates Reservation and Ticketing office.

For [extending and reinstating Skywards Miles](/english/skywards/do-more-with-your-miles/), you can only do it online by logging in on emirates.com.

  * ### How do I buy Skywards Miles for myself or as a gift for someone else?

__

If you haven’t earned enough Skywards Miles to achieve the reward of your choice, or you’d like to give Skywards Miles to a fellow Emirates Skywards member as a gift, you can buy them online by logging in and visiting this[page](/english/skywards/do-more-with-your-miles/). A purchasing member’s account must have at least one Emirates flight or partner earning activity.

    * Platinum and Gold members can purchase up to 200,000 Skywards Miles in a calendar year
    * Silver and Blue members can purchase up to 100,000 Skywards Miles in a calendar year
    * At least 2,000 Skywards Miles must be purchased or gifted per transaction, priced at USD30 for every 1,000 Skywards Miles

  * ### Can I buy or gift any number of Skywards Miles?

__

Skywards Miles can be purchased for yourself or gifted to someone else in multiples of 1,000, at a minimum amount of 2,000 Skywards Miles.

    * Platinum and Gold members can purchase up to 200,000 Skywards Miles in a calendar year for self through the Buy Miles product and receive as a gift through the Gift Miles product
    * Silver and Blue members can purchase up to 100,000 Skywards Miles in a calendar year for self through the Buy Miles product and receive as a gift through the Gift Miles product

Visit this [page](/english/skywards/do-more-with-your-miles/) for more information.

  * ### Is buying or gifting Skywards Miles equivalent to buying or gifting an Emirates Voucher?

__

No. Bought or gifted Skywards Miles can be used for Classic Rewards flight or Upgrade redemption on an existing Emirates or flydubai ticket. The amount paid for the bought or gifted Skywards Miles cannot be used as a cash voucher for Emirates products and services.

  * ### What can I redeem my bought or gifted Skywards Miles for?

__

The Skywards Miles you Buy or Gift can be redeemed for Classic Rewards flights and Upgrades redemption. While we don’t restrict spending your Skywards Miles on any products or services offered by Emirates, we encourage you to check the Skywards Miles requirement for flights and upgrades on our[Miles Calculator](/english/skywards/miles-calculator/).

  * ### If I don’t have enough Skywards Miles for a flight reward, can I buy more?

__

Yes, you can buy more if you have insufficient Skywards Miles to avail a flight reward. Read the '[How do I buy Skywards Miles](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/how-do-i-buy-skywards-miles-for-myself-or-as-a-gift-for-someone-else/)' FAQ for more information or log in and visit the [Buy Skywards Miles](/english/skywards/do-more-with-your-miles/) page.

If you would like to check how many Miles would you need for a flight reward to one of our destinations, you can check through our [Miles Calculator](/english/skywards/miles-calculator/).

  * ### Can I transfer Skywards Miles from one account to another?

__

Yes, you can transfer Skywards Miles to another Emirates Skywards account. Simply log in to[emirates.com](https://www.emirates.com/ "emirates.com") and go to the Transfer Skywards Miles from this [page](/english/skywards/do-more-with-your-miles/), or use the Emirates app and visit the Skywards section. Selected Emirates retail stores and the [Emirates Contact Centre](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/) can also assist you with the process.

Here are key details to remember:

    * Ensure that you have the recipient’s details at the time of the transfer.
    * The receiving account must have at least one Emirates flight or partner earning activity to be eligible.
    * You can transfer up to 50,000 Skywards Miles per calendar year, priced at USD15 for every 1,000 Skywards Miles. Each transaction requires a minimum of 2,000 Skywards Miles.

  * ### Can I transfer any number of Skywards Miles?

__

Skywards Miles can be transferred in multiples of 1,000, beginning at 2,000 Skywards Miles, and you can transfer up to 50,000 Skywards Miles to another Emirates Skywards member, or members, in one calendar year.

  * ### How long are transferred Skywards Miles valid for?

__

Transferred Skywards Miles are valid for a minimum of 3 years from the date of transfer and will expire at the end of the receiving member’s month of birth on the third year.

  * ### Once I transfer Skywards Miles, can I reverse the transaction?

__

Unfortunately, we cannot transfer Skywards Miles back to your account once you have decided to transfer them to another member.

  * ### Can I pay to extend Skywards Miles in my account that are due to expire?

__

Yes. If you have any Skywards Miles in your account that are due to expire in the next 3 months, you can pay to extend their validity for another 12 months beyond the date of the original expiry.

Extension of Skywards Miles is available at a lower price than our standard Buy Skywards Miles product. 

You can extend a minimum of 1,000 Skywards Miles and a maximum of 50,000 Skywards Miles per calendar year.

Visit this [page](/english/skywards/do-more-with-your-miles/) for more information.

  * ### Can I pay to re-instate Skywards Miles that have expired?

__

Yes, Skywards Miles which have expired may be reinstated so long as the request is made within 6 months of expiry. Any Skywards Miles reinstated will be valid for 12 months beyond the date of reinstatement.

Reinstatement of Skywards Miles is available at a lower price than our standard Buy Miles offer. 

You can reinstate a minimum of 1,000 Skywards Miles and a maximum of 50,000 Miles per calendar year.

  * ### What forms of payment are available to buy, gift, transfer, extend or reinstate Skywards Miles?

__

Payment for transactions made to buy, gift, transfer, extend and reinstate Skywards Miles can be made with major debit and credit cards. Payment is not available using cash.

  * ### Can I Buy, Gift, Transfer, Reinstate, Extend or Multiply Skywards Miles from our My Family account?

__

These services are currently only available to a member using an individual Emirates Skywards account and do not apply to My Family accounts. Which means additional Skywards Miles can’t be purchased for My Family accounts and can’t be gifted, transferred or reinstated.

  * ### How will my buy, gift, transfer, extend or reinstate Skywards Miles transaction reflect on my credit card statement?

__

Your transaction will be conducted with Points.com which is a fully authorised and approved partner of the Emirates Skywards programme. When you receive your credit card statement it will read ‘Skywards Miles by PTS' and the amount of the purchase.

Visit this [page](/english/skywards/do-more-with-your-miles/) for more information.

## Claiming Miles

  * ### If I am newly enrolled, can I claim Miles for any flights I have taken recently?

__

Yes, new members can claim Miles for Emirates, flydubai, and Qantas flights flown up to two months prior to registering with Emirates Skywards.

However, any other transaction, like flights with our other partner airlines or purchases of partner services and products, made before you registered won’t be eligible for earning or accruing Miles.

  * ### How do I claim missing Miles from flydubai?

__

If you’re missing Miles for flydubai flights, please log in and submit an online claim on flydubai.com.

  * ### How do I claim missing Miles from Emirates?

__

If you’re missing Miles for an Emirates flight, please log in and submit an[online claim](/english/skywards/account/my-statement/claim-miles/ "Claim Miles"). Miles can be claimed only for qualifying flights taken within six months from the travel date. We’ll credit the Miles into your account straight away, as long as the name on the ticket matches the name in your Emirates Skywards profile exactly.

  * ### How do I claim missing Miles from an Emirates Skywards partner?

__

You can submit a claim if your Miles haven’t been credited to your account within three weeks of the partner transaction date. To claim missing Miles, the name used for the partner booking must match the name in your Emirates Skywards profile exactly. Depending on the partner, follow one of these steps to claim your Miles:

    1. **Airlines:** contact us via [Live Chat](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/)* and provide the required information such as booking name, flight date, flight code, class of travel, origin, destination and ticket number. 
    2. **Hotels, car rental or retail and lifestyle:** contact us via [Live Chat](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/)* and be ready with a copy of the original invoices within six months of the transaction date. Please note some of our partners offer the ability to claim missing Miles directly from their website, including [Avis﻿ __](https://www.avisworld.com/)[(Opens an external website in a new tab) ﻿ __](https://www.hertz.com/rentacar/partner/index.jsp?targetPage=ftRetroFormView.jsp),[ Hertz(Opens an external website in a new tab)﻿ __](https://www.hertz.com/rentacar/partner/index.jsp?targetPage=ftRetroFormView.jsp),[ Europcar(Opens an external website in a new tab)﻿ __](https://www.europcar.com/en-us/p/europcar-partner/emirates-skywards), and[ Sixt(Opens an external website in a new tab)﻿ __](https://www.sixt.com/#/?page=loginregister&redirectPath=%2Faccount%2F%23%2Faccount).
    3. **Banks:** please contact your bank’s service centre directly. 

Please allow six to eight weeks from the date your claim is received for any missing Miles to appear in your account. 

Some of our partners offer the facility to make a claim directly on their website. You can check if this service is available by visiting the individual partner page. 

 _*Live chat is currently available in English only._

  * ### How do I claim Miles for My Family account?

__

If you’re missing Miles from an Emirates flight, please log in and submit an[online claim](/english/skywards/account/my-statement/claim-miles/ "Claim Miles"). 

We’ll credit the Miles into your account straight away, as long as the name on the ticket matches the name in your Emirates Skywards profile exactly. To credit Miles into your My Family account, you have to quote your individual membership number. Based on the contribution percentage you have chosen, the Miles will be credited back to your My Family account. 

Please note that My Family members cannot make backdated claims for flights they have taken before joining My Family.

  * ### How do Skysurfers claim missing Miles?

__

To claim missing Miles on a Skysurfers account, the nominated parent or guardian can simply visit this[page](/english/skywards/claim-miles/ "Claim Miles ") and follow the steps based on whether the claim is for Emirates flights, flydubai flights, or any of our other partners.

  * ### Why are Miles missing from my account?

__

Miles might be missing from your statement for several reasons. The most common are:

    1. The name on the reservation doesn’t exactly match the name registered on your Emirates Skywards profile. 
    2. The transaction is still being processed (please allow 48 hours for a flight booked with Emirates or flydubai, or up to three weeks for a transaction with an Emirates Skywards partner). 
    3. Your Emirates Skywards membership number was not quoted, or was misquoted, at the time of booking or at check-in. 
    4. You have not travelled on the inbound or outbound part of your journey yet

## Earning Miles with Emirates and flydubai

  * ### What are base Miles?

__

Base Miles are the standard Skywards Miles earned on any Emirates ticket, without any kind of Bonus Miles*.

The number of Miles you earn depends on the fare type of your ticket. The baseline used to calculate standard Skywards Miles is Economy Flex Plus for Emirates flights and Economy Flex for flydubai flights. This is why other fare types earn more or fewer Miles.

You can use our [Miles Calculator](/english/skywards/miles-calculator/) to check the total Miles you’ll earn on an Emirates ticket. Total Miles are made up of base Miles for your origin and destination, plus the various cabin class and tier bonuses on offer. 

 _*Bonus Miles are additional Skywards Miles that members earn when they travel in premium cabins (Business Class and First Class) and/or if they are Silver, Gold, or Platinum members._

  * ### What is a fare and how does it influence the number of Miles that I will earn?

__

The fare is the price paid for your ticket. Each cabin have different fare types.

On Emirates flights:

    1. Economy and Business Class: Special, Saver, Flex or Flex Plus
    2. Premium Economy: Flex Plus
    3. First Class: Flex or Flex Plus

On flydubai flights:

    1. Economy Class: Lite, Value, Flex 
    2. Business Class: Business

The fare type you choose will influence the number of Miles you will earn.

  * ### Do I earn Miles whatever the fare type?

__

Yes, you do. You’ll earn both Skywards Miles and Tier Miles on all fare types in every cabin. The number of Miles you earn depends on your fare type. To see how many Miles you can earn, check out our[Miles Calculator](/english/skywards/miles-calculator/).

  * ### Why do the Miles I earn different for each fare type?

__

We recognise that different customers can pay different fares while travelling in the same cabin, so when we calculate the Miles you earn, we take into account the type of fare as well as the distance flown. Customers choose different fare types based on their travel needs. Along with the distance flown, the fare type helps determine how many Miles you earn - so we can recognise the added cost of the fare you've selected for your journey.

  * ### Why should I buy a Flex or Flex Plus fare when a cheaper fare is available?

__

Our Special and Saver fares are our most affordable fares, but Flex and Flex Plus fares offer extra benefits:

    * You’ll earn more Skywards and Tier Miles on a Flex or Flex Plus fare, so you can reach your next reward or the next tier faster.
    * You also have more flexibility to change or cancel your ticket
    * You need fewer Skywards Miles to upgrade to a higher cabin class.

If you’re travelling in Economy Class on a Flex or Flex Plus fare, you won’t have to pay for [Seat Selection](/english/book/about-booking-online/emirates-seat-selection/).

  * ### How do I identify a ticket’s fare type before I purchase it?

__

The fare type will be clearly displayed when you search for flights on emirates.com or flydubai.com. It will show the price, fare conditions and the Miles that you will earn. If you are logged in as an Emirates Skywards member, you can even view flight-specific bonuses.

  * ### Does cabin class determine the ticket’s fare type?

__

No, fare types are not restricted by the class you travel in. When you are searching for or booking a flight, you will see which types of fares are available.

Read this [FAQ](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/what-is-a-fare-and-how-does-it-influence-the-number-of-miles-that-i-will-earn/) to know more about what fare types are available in each cabin class.

  * ### What Miles bonuses do Silver, Gold or Platinum members earn?

__

When flying Emirates or flydubai, Silver members receive 30% bonus Skywards Miles, Gold members receive 75% bonus Skywards Miles and Platinum members receive 100% bonus.

On Emirates flights, the bonus is calculated based on the Miles earned at the Economy Flex Plus level for that journey.

On flydubai flights, the bonus is calculated based on the fare brand purchased for the journey.

  * ### Do I earn bonus Miles when I travel in Premium Cabins?

__

When travelling in either Emirates Business Class, Emirates First Class, or flydubai Business Class, you will earn additional bonus Skywards and Tier Miles. To check the number of Miles you will earn when travelling in premium cabins, visit our[Miles Calculator](/english/skywards/miles-calculator/).

  * ### If I have a mixed class or mixed fare ticket, what Miles will I receive?

__

If your ticket is split between different fare types, you will earn a different number of Miles for each part of your journey that is booked on a different fare.

  * ### If I upgrade on board with cash, will I be awarded additional Miles as per the upgraded class?

__

No, Skywards Members will earn Miles as per the original ticketed class of travel. No additional Miles will be awarded to the member in case of on board upgrades paid for with cash.

  * ### Do I earn Miles on Classic Rewards?

__

No, Classic Reward tickets are not eligible to accrue Skywards Miles and Tier Miles because these are redemption flights - you’re using Miles instead of earning them this time.

  * ### Do I earn Miles for my upgraded cabin if I redeem an Upgrade Reward?

__

No, you won’t earn Skywards Miles and Tier Miles for your upgraded cabin if you’ve used your Miles to purchase an upgrade. If your original booking was paid in cash, your Miles will be earned based on the original cabin you booked, not the upgraded one.

  * ### Will I earn Skywards Miles and Tier Miles on my flight if I used Cash+Miles?

__

You’ll earn Skywards Miles and Tier Miles on the part of your ticket that you pay for in cash, excluding carrier-imposed charges, taxes and fees. The rate will depend on the type of ticket you have bought.

Earning on other FFP/loyalty programmes is not available. You will also not earn Skywards Miles or Tier Miles on any flight-related product or service you paid for using Cash+Miles.

  * ### When will Miles be credited to my account?

__

Miles are credited to your account after you’ve physically flown from your origin airport to your destination airport. They are credited in two stages, firstly when you have finished the outbound part of your trip and again when you have completed the inbound voyage. So, if you fly from London to Sydney return, you are credited Miles once you arrive in Sydney and again when you return to London.

  * ### Do stopovers affect how Miles are credited?

__

Stopovers have no effect on the amount of Miles earned and are not counted as a destination. So, if you stopover in Dubai on your way to Sydney from London, you would still only receive your Miles credit once you arrive in Sydney.

  * ### If I don't complete my journey, when will my Miles be credited?

__

If you do not complete all your ticketed flights (for instance if part of your ticket is refunded or voided), we will credit Miles for any flights you have flown as soon as you submit the remainder of your ticket for cancellation or refund.[Emirates Contact Centre](/english/help/ "Help and support") can assist you with this.

  * ### Can I decide which account I want to earn Miles with after I’ve travelled?

__

No. You must decide which programme you wish to earn Miles on at the time of reservation or check-in for Qualifying Flights and at the time of paying for other qualifying goods or services. No changes can be made to the membership number once a member has checked in for the first flight of an itinerary.

## Membership Tiers and Benefits

  * ### What are the benefits of each of the different membership tiers?

__

Each Emirates Skywards membership tier comes with a range of benefits that members look forward to. As a member, you can enjoy perks such as onboard Wi-Fi, instant upgrades, airport lounge access, bonus Miles when you fly, and much more.

To see the full list of benefits for each tier, visit our [Membership Benefits](/english/skywards/membership-benefits/) page.

  * ### Will having a digital card change my membership benefits?

__

No. We are always working to ensure that our members have as seamless a journey as possible. As part of this, we have removed the need for you to possess or present a physical membership card – giving you one less thing to remember when you travel.

Giving you a digital version of the card provides a more convenient and seamless way for you to access your membership details. You can log in, go to ‘My Overview’, scroll down to ‘Quick Links’, and click on [Membership Card](/english/skywards/account/my-profile/membership-card/) – add it to your Apple Wallet, print it, or save it to your device’s photo or image library for quick access.

  * ### How can I ensure that I keep my current Tier?

__

Your first tier review takes place 12 months after you move into a new tier.

During the review period of 12 months, you should have met the below for your Tier.

Silver Tier: 25,000 Tier Miles

Gold Tier: 50,000 Tier Miles

Platinum Tier: 150,000 Tier Miles and at least one qualifying flight in First Class or Business Class

If you’ve met the Tier Miles required for your current tier, you’ll retain your status. If you fall short, you’ll be downgraded.

Each time your Tier is reviewed and retained, the next review will be automatically scheduled 12 months from the date you qualified.

  * ### How do I move up to a higher tier?

__

We assess if you’re ready to move up a tier every time you earn Tier Miles, so you may be assessed multiple times a year. To move up to the next tier, you need to earn enough Tier Miles in the last 12 months, which is your assessment period.

    * To reach Silver membership, you need to have 25,000 Tier Miles.
    * To reach Gold membership, you need to have 50,000 Tier Miles.
    * To reach Platinum membership, you need to have 150,000 Tier Miles and at least one qualifying flight in First Class or Business Class.

Please check your [My Overview](/english/skywards/account/my-overview/) page for information about your tier membership and key review dates. You don’t need to apply to move up a tier, we will automatically move you to the next tier when you’ve earned enough Tier Miles.

  * ### How can you reach the next tier faster?

__

To reach the next tier faster, fly with Emirates and flydubai - the more you fly, the more Tier Miles you earn.

The number of Tier Miles you earn depends on the fare type within your chosen cabin class. Higher fare types, such as Flex and Flex Plus, generally earn more Miles and help you reach your next tier faster. To know more about what fare types are available in each cabin class, you can visit this [page](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/what-is-a-fare-and-how-does-it-influence-the-number-of-miles-that-i-will-earn/).

Additionally, if you subscribe to Skywards+ Premium package, you earn 20% more Tier Miles during your Skywards+ subscription period. Visit the [Skywards+](/english/skywards/skywards-plus/subscription/ "Subscribe now") page to know more.

  * ### How long are the benefits valid for in my current Tier?

__

You enjoy your membership privileges for 12 months.

For example, if you achieve Silver membership on 15 October 2026, your tier review date will be 31 October 2027. This means you can use your Silver Tier benefits until end of October 2027.

Tier reviews always take place at the end of every month.

  * ### What happens to my Skywards Miles once I downgrade from Platinum to Gold?

__

If and when you downgrade from Platinum to Gold, any unredeemed Skywards Miles that were extended on account of you being a Platinum member, will automatically expire.

Whenever you redeem Miles for a reward, the Miles deducted from your account will always be the ones that have been in your account the longest. This helps to minimise any chance of losing your Miles.

  * ### Can I use my Skywards Miles to purchase Silver, Gold or Platinum membership?

__

No. Tier status can only be earned by accumulating[Tier Miles](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/what-are-tier-miles/).

__

You won’t earn additional Tier Miles for being a Silver, Gold or Platinum member. However, you can earn extra Tier Miles by travelling Business Class or First Class or choosing a Flex or Flex Plus fare. Additionally, if you subscribe to Skywards+ Premium package, you earn 20% more Tier Miles during your Skywards+ subscription period. Visit the[Skywards+](/english/skywards/skywards-plus/subscription/) page to know more.

  * ### Who is eligible for Emirates Skywards luggage tags?

Silver, Gold and Platinum members are eligible for two personalised luggage tags per tier cycle. Skywards Skysurfers members are not eligible for luggage tags.

Silver, Gold and Platinum members can get their luggage tags printed at the Business Class lounges at Dubai Airport Terminal 3. Platinum members will continue to receive their packs along with their personalised luggage tags.

If you’re an Emirates Skywards Silver or Gold member, you can collect your tags from the Skywards Team at Dubai Airport (Business class lounges in all concourses and Skywards Centre Duty free level concourse B). If you’re a Platinum member, you will continue to receive your luggage tags in a Skywards pack couriered to you.

You can request your tags at any point during your tier cycle.

  * ### What is the No Miles Expiry benefit available to Platinum Tier members?

__

Effective 30 November 2018, any Skywards Miles belonging to a Platinum member will not expire for as long he/she retains membership of the Platinum tier. If you are a Platinum member, you will see an adjusted expiry date whenever you have any Skywards Miles that were originally due to expire during your current Platinum tier cycle. This adjusted date will show as three (3) months after your next Platinum tier review date.

For example: if a Platinum member (with next tier review date of 31 December 2026) has Skywards Miles due to originally expire on 31 July 2026 as per standard expiry, this member will see an adjusted expiry date of 31 March 2027 (calculated as 3 months after the upcoming tier review date).

Similarly, when a Platinum member retains their Platinum membership for another year, any unused Skywards Miles that were extended in their last Platinum cycle will again be extended to three (3) months after their next Platinum tier review date. The only time Skywards Miles that were extended on account of the member being Platinum will expire is if and when a member downgrades to Gold and has yet to redeem such Miles. You can refer to the [Emirates Skywards Programme Rules](/english/skywards/emirates-skywards-programme-rules/) for complete details.

You can invite any members of your immediate family to join. If they’re not already Emirates Skywards members, they’ll just need to register first before you can add them. ## Skysurfers

  * ### What is Skywards Skysurfers?

__

It’s our club for young frequent flyers aged between 2 and 17. Members earn Miles with Emirates, flydubai and our partners in the same ways and at the same rate as the Emirates Skywards programme. Skysurfers can redeem their Skywards Miles for reward flights or a variety of exciting rewards, with the approval of their registered parent or guardian. For more details, please visit the[Skywards Skysurfers](/english/skywards/skysurfers/) page.

  * ### What are the benefits of Skywards Skysurfers?

__

The benefits are similar to the Emirates Skywards programme. A Skysurfers can achieve Silver or Gold status, and enjoy the extra benefits of that tier, in exactly the same way as an Emirates Skywards member. However, Skysurfers are not eligible for Platinum membership.

Skywards Skysurfers Silver members: 

    * Eligibility – Emirates Business Class Lounge access only in Dubai for self ONLY if accompanied by an adult (over 18) who is eligible to access the lounge in their own right. NO guest access allowed. 

Skywards Skysurfers Gold members: 

    * Eligibility – Emirates Business Class Lounge access in Dubai and across the network for self + one guest who must be an adult (over 18) OR who is eligible to access the lounge in their own right.

  * ### How do young flyers enrol in Skywards Skysurfers?

__

Enrolling young flyers as Skywards Skysurfers is easy:

    1. Parents or guardians log in to their Emirates Skywards account on the Emirates website. 
    2. Go to the Skysurfers page or MyFamily page and [add their child’s details](/english/skywards/my-family/skysurfer-registration/) to enrol them as a Skywards Skysurfer. 

Once enrolled, the child’s account will remain linked to the parent or guardian’s personal account until they turn 18. During this period, only one registered parent or guardian can manage the Skysurfer’s account.

  * ### What membership tiers does Skywards Skysurfers have?

__

Skysurfers also start from Blue and can move up to Silver and Gold tiers in exactly the same way as Emirates Skywards members. However, there is no equivalent Platinum tier for Skysurfers.

  * ### Can I book a reward online using Skywards Miles from a Skysurfer or a minor’s account?

__

Yes, however, this online functionality is only available to the registered parent/guardian who is an Emirates Skywards member and have their child’s account[linked to their account](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/how-do-i-link-a-skysurfer-or-a-minor-s-account-to-my-skywards-account/). Once you are logged in to your account on emirates.com, you can view a drop down list that allows you to select from account numbers before making the reward booking.

  * ### How do I link a Skysurfer or a minor’s account to my Skywards account?

__

If you already have a My Family account, you can simply add your child as a Family Member. You have to be the Family Head in the My Family account, your child has to already be a Skywards Skysurfers member and you are the registered parent/guardian managing their account for you to add them.

  * ### How do Skysurfers redeem Skywards Miles for Emirates rewards or partner rewards?

__

Skywards Skysurfers can spend their Skywards Miles on Emirates flights and with selected airline partners. If you’ve linked the Skysurfers member’s account to yours and you are the registered parent/guardian managing the account, you can choose which account to spend Skywards Miles from. You can also[chat](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/) with us or call your local [Emirates Contact Centre](/english/help/ "Help and support") if you need help with booking your flight. First Class Classic Rewards and Reward Upgrades from Business to First Class are only available for passengers aged 9 years old and above.

  * ### What is the No Miles Expiry benefit available to Skywards Skysurfers members?

__

Effective from 1 April 2024, any Skywards Miles held in a Skysurfers’s account shall not expire for as long as they are a Skysurfers. Once a Skysurfers turns 18 and becomes a Skywards Member, Skywards Miles from their Skysurfers account shall expire on the last day of the month in which they turn 21 years old. You can refer to Skywards Skysurfers section Clause 3.5 of the[Emirates Skywards Programme Rules](/english/skywards/emirates-skywards-programme-rules/ "Emirates Skywards Programme Rules") for full details.

  * ### Can Skysurfers buy or transfer Skywards Miles?

__

Skysurfers cannot Buy, Gift, Transfer, Reinstate or Extend expired Skywards Miles in their own right. They are also not eligible to receive Miles via the Gift or Transfer of Skywards Miles option.

  * ### What happens to Skywards Skysurfers members when they turn 18?

__

Once Skysurfers turns 18 years old they will be given the opportunity to transition their Account into an individual Account managed solely by the Member, in which case the registered parent/guardian shall no longer have access to the Member’s Account. To complete the transition, the Member will need to call the[Emirates Contact Centre](/english/help/ "Help and support") or use the [live chat](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/) function on the Website. The Member will need to provide the relevant Emirates Contact Centre agent with (i) their Account membership number, and (ii) a new unique email address for the Account, to reset their Account password and create their new Account login credentials.

  * ### What happens to a Skywards Skysurfers member's Tier status when they turn 18?

__

When Skysurfers turn 18, their account converts to a standard Emirates Skywards account.

Their Tier status will be based on the Tier Miles accumulated in their account at the time of transition. During the review period of 12 months, they should have met the below for their Tier: 

Silver Tier: 25,000 Tier Miles 

Gold Tier: 50,000 Tier Miles 

Gold Tier: 150,000 Tier Miles with no qualifying flight in First Class or Business Class 

Platinum Tier: 150,000 Tier Miles and at least one qualifying flight in First Class or Business Class

## Spending Miles with Emirates and flydubai

  * ### What is a Classic Reward?

__

Classic Reward is a flight reward paid fully in Skywards Miles on Emirates, flydubai or Emirates Skywards partner airlines. Taxes will always have to be paid in cash.

For a Classic Reward on Emirates, you also have the option to pay the carrier-imposed charges using Skywards Miles.

If you would like to check how many Skywards Miles would you need for a Classic Reward with Emirates, you can check through our [Miles Calculator](/english/skywards/miles-calculator/). 

  * ### When I use Skywards Miles to buy a reward ticket, will I have to pay any extra charges?

__

Yes, in addition to the fare paid in Skywards Miles you will be charged carrier-imposed charges and airport and government taxes. These charges would differ based on the Airline (Emirates, Qantas, etc.) being booked on.

  * ### Can I book a one-way flight reward?

__

Yes, you can. However, when it comes to one-way flights, there are certain restrictions based on your class of travel.

On Emirates flights: 

    * For Economy, you can book one-way classic reward in Saver and Flex Plus.
    * For Business, you can only book one-way classic reward in Flex Plus.

On flydubai flights: 

    * For Economy, you can book one-way classic reward in Saver and Flex Plus.
    * For Business, you can book one-way classic reward in Saver.

You can book these tickets for one-way flights using a classic reward, as long as you fly from one flight zone to another. They’re not valid for travel within a single zone. You can add one stopover on your journey, but your journey must take place in one continuous direction. This one-way ticket will require 50% of the Skywards Miles needed for a return ticket between the same airports.

  * ### Can I add stopovers when booking a one-way Classic Reward ticket?

__

Yes, but your journey must move in one continuous direction (no backtracking), and it must take you from one flight zone to another. Traveling within the same zone isn’t permitted for Classic Rewards.

  * ### How is pricing calculated for a one-way Classic Reward compared to a return ticket?

__

A one-way Classic Reward ticket is priced at 50% of the Skywards Miles needed for a Flex Plus return ticket between the same two airports. It's a straightforward half-mile calculation based on the return Flex Plus rate.

  * ### Where can I make changes to a Classic Reward ticket?

__

For flydubai Classic Reward tickets, please contact your local[Emirates Contact Centre](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/) to make any changes to your booking.

For Emirates Classic Rewards tickets, you can make changes online through the [Manage your booking](/english/manage-booking/ "Manage your booking") page or by calling your local [Emirates Contact Centre](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/). 

  * ### Do I need to purchase flight rewards for infants? 

__

To enjoy reward flights with your family, you only need to spend 10% of the Skywards Miles value of an adult reward ticket for infants below two years old. Please note that you can only redeem Classic Rewards for infants in Economy Class.

You can enjoy upgrade rewards using Skywards Miles for infants from Economy Class to Premium Economy, Economy Class to Business Class, and Premium Economy to Business Class. Upgrade rewards from Business to First Class are only available for passengers aged 9 years old and above.

  * ### Can I book a Classic Reward in First Class for my children?

__

Yes, but please note that Classic Rewards in First Class can only be booked for children aged 9 years old and above.

  * ### Can I spend my Skywards Miles on flight rewards for friends and family?

__

Yes, you can. Booking an Emirates flight reward for someone else follows the same process as booking for yourself. Simply visit Emirates’ online booking page, toggle the Classic Rewards option, and enter the passenger details accordingly.

For partner flight rewards, please refer to this [FAQ](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/can-i-book-flight-rewards-and-upgrades-online/) (flydubai) and this [FAQ](/english/help/faq-topics/emirates-skywards/faq/how-can-i-book-flight-rewards-with-a-partner-airline/) (other airline partners) for more information on the booking process.

  * ### As a Blue member, can I book a Classic Reward in First Class?

__

No, only Emirates Skywards Silver, Gold and Platinum members can book First Class Classic Rewards as part of their tier benefits. You can reach Silver by earning Tier Miles on flights with Emirates and flydubai.

  * ### What is the difference between a Saver and Flex Plus flight reward?

__

You can read the full details in the Classic Reward Section (Section 12) of our[Programme Rules](/english/skywards/emirates-skywards-programme-rules/).

Saver rewards offer the best value when you travel during lower demand periods or when your travel dates are flexible.

    * You can book them on return flights in Business Class and on both one-way and return flights in Economy Class. When booking a return flight reward, you can choose a return date up to 3 months after your outbound travel date. You can add one stopover to your journey.
    * You can change your travel dates after the ticket has been issued for USD 25 per change
    * A fee of USD 75 will apply if you cancel an unused Saver reward ticket.

Flex Plus rewards offer you even more reward seats during high demand periods and on high demand flights.

    * You can book them on both one-way and return flights in every class of travel.
    * When booking a return flight reward, you can choose a return date up to 12 months after your outbound travel date.
    * You can add two stopovers to your journey.
    * You can make changes to your travel dates at any time without charge.
    * You can cancel your ticket without charge before travel.

  * ### Can I combine Saver and Flex Plus rewards?

__

Yes, you can book a return ticket using a combination of Saver and Flex Plus reward fares, depending on your travel requirements and what allocated seats are available at the time of booking.

  * ### What if I can’t redeem my Skywards Miles on a high-demand flight?

__

High demand flights mean less available seats. The next time you plan on traveling during peak travel season, it’s best to choose a Flex Plus reward. They cost more Skywards Miles than a Saver reward, but they are available for redemption on a wider range of travel dates.

  * ### Why am I asked for a credit card when buying a flight with my Skywards Miles?

__

Credit card information is required in order to cover the government taxes and airport charges levied on all ticket purchases, including those purchased with Skywards Miles, whether with Emirates or with a partner airline. Airport taxes are compulsory and are not taken by the airline but are usually charged by governments or airport management companies.

  * ### If a classic reward is available in a higher cabin, will an upgrade reward from a lower cabin to the same higher cabin also be available?

Not always. Classic rewards and upgrade rewards are two different flight rewards with different allocations and availability rules, so one being available doesn’t guarantee the other will be.

    * Flex and Flex+ fares: As long as there are seats available, you can redeem your upgrade online any time up to six hours before departure. Flex requires fewer Skywards Miles than Saver, and Flex+ even fewer.

The Skywards Miles needed for an Emirates Flight upgrade reflect the type of fare that you buy. For example, you need fewer Skywards Miles to upgrade a Flex Plus or Flex fare compared to a Saver fare.

Yes. During booking, you’ll see which tickets are eligible for upgrades, along with the number of Skywards Miles required.

__

There are two ways to book a flight reward with our partner airlines, depending on which airline you’re flying with:

    1. For some partners, you can book directly through Emirates’ online booking system. Select ‘Classic Rewards’, choose ‘Partner’ under the ‘Airline’ dropdown, and enter your flight details.
    2. For other partners, you’ll need to contact us via [Live Chat](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/) to complete the booking.

To find out which booking method applies to each partner, please visit our [Partners](/english/skywards/partners/ "Our Partners") page.

  * ### Can I upgrade using my Skywards Miles on a partner airline or on a codeshare flight?

__

Unfortunately, this is currently not possible. You can only upgrade on flights marketed and operated by Emirates.

  * ### How do I find all the Emirates Skywards partners’ special offers?

__

You can find all our latest partner offers by category on our[Special Offers](/english/special-offers/) page. We’re always adding new deals, so it’s worth checking in regularly - you might find something for your next trip.

  * ### Can I book tickets on codeshare or partner airlines using Cash+Miles?

__

Yes, Cash+Miles payment is available when you book Emirates marketed flights, including those operated by our codeshare partners.

  * ### Can I cancel or modify my Partner Reward?

__

Yes, you can cancel and/or modify most Partner Rewards, depending on availability, and charges may apply. Start a[Live Chat](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/)* with us to learn more. 

 _*Live chat is currently available in English only._

  * ### Can reward tickets be refunded or endorsed to other airlines?

__

Yes, you can refund any unused Classic Reward ticket, but you can’t endorse it to another airline. If you’re travelling on a Classic Reward ticket and your flight has been cancelled, you will be transferred to the next available flight or endorsed to another airline at the discretion of Emirates.

  * ### Can I redeem Miles on Qantas-operated flights?

__

Yes, you can redeem your Skywards Miles for flights operated by Qantas (QF flight code). You can book your reward with Qantas on Emirates.com by visiting our[Qantas partnership page](/english/skywards/partners/qantas/). If you are unable to access our online booking service, please reach out to us through the [Emirates Contact Centre](https://www.emirates.com/english/help/skywards-web-chat/).

  * ### Where can I use my Skywards Miles?

__

You can spend Skywards Miles on future trips, hotel stays, live events, exclusive invitations and more. Visit the[Spend Miles page](/english/skywards/spend-miles/) for more details.

  * ### Can I upgrade with Skywards Miles on Qantas-operated flights?

__

Unfortunately, this option does not apply to Qantas flights.
